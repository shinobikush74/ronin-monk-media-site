Long bio (180 words)

Ronin Monk Media LLC is an independent, Black-owned publishing and audio house founded by Shinobi Kush — writer, producer, and architect of a single sprawling mythos. Across novellas, codex, and original score, the house tells one story: the Sky Khan Doctrines, a cosmic civilizational saga where Afro-futurist sacred technology is the source civilization — not a stylistic overlay. Eight rival factions with their own proprietary visual physics span seven eras of harmonic warfare, ashfall, and reawakening. Releases include the Ronin Monk Sagas, the Books of the Frequency Wars, Tablets of Lutz, and Neo Mycelium: the Digital Ascension of Shinobi Kush — the last paired with Static Before Sound, the long-form audio compendium that scores the canon. The studio also maintains the Sky Khan Visual Engine, a custom prompt and codex toolchain that enforces civilizational physics across every image, page, and track. The work threads ancestral memory, sacred technology, and cinematic mythmaking — built to be walked into for years.
