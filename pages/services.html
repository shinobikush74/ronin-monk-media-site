Narrative Brand Audit

The deliverable is a PDF report: what is working, what is missing, and rewrite recommendations with examples. No vague feedback — specific language, specific fixes.

  • PDF report delivered within 5 business days

Ideal for

Before You Reach Out

Common questions

How do I book a service?

Use the contact form. Include which service you're interested in and a brief description of your project. We respond within 3 business days.

How do I pay?

Payment is processed securely via PayPal. Click any booking button to go directly to checkout. Prefer to discuss scope first? Reach out via the contact form and we'll send you a PayPal invoice.

How long does a project take?

Worldbuilding consultations are single 60-minute sessions. Brand audits deliver within 5 business days. Ghostwriting timelines are set in the scoping call based on word count and complexity.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. All sessions are remote. All deliverables are digital. Location is not a constraint.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Every engagement includes revision rounds. If the deliverable is substantially off from what was agreed, we'll discuss a remedy. We stand behind the work.

Can I hire you for something not listed here?

Yes. Reach out and describe what you need. If it falls within narrative, worldbuilding, or long-form writing, we may be able to help.
