Guardians of Cosmic Harmony

Ronin Monk Sagas

A solo wanderer on a switchback ridge, halo-rings dim across the shoulders, listening for the broken signal of a fallen age. Indigo circuit-cloth drifting in mountain wind, brass prayer-bells at the hip.

The Ronin physics

The Ronin move slow and ceremonial. Their cloth drifts. Mist parts at the shoulder. Architecture is the switchback stair, the shrine confrontation, the broken-gate contemplation. Scale means: humans small against sacred vastness.

The arc

Each saga is a single recovered transmission — a moment from one wanderer's pilgrimage between the ruined shrines of the Golden Harmonic Era and the ash-buried relics of the Interregnum. Some sagas are confrontations. Most are quiet. None are generic.
