Accessibility

This site targets WCAG 2.1 AA conformance and is built deliberately for keyboard, screen reader, and low-vision users.

What's in place

  • Semantic landmarks (header, main, nav, footer).
  • Skip-to-content link, visible focus rings, and keyboard-operable navigation.
  • Color contrast verified at AA minimums against the obsidian palette.
  • prefers-reduced-motion respected — parallax and fade-ins disabled.
  • All embed regions labeled; form fields paired with labels.
