Common Questions

What you need to know

What does Ronin Monk Media LLC do?

We are an independent Afro-futurist publishing and creative studio. We publish novellas, audio compendiums, and visual codex — and offer worldbuilding consultation, narrative brand audits, and ghostwriting to other creators.

Can I hire you for my project?

Yes. We offer worldbuilding consultation ($125/session), narrative brand audits ($275 flat), and ghostwriting (from $0.12/word). See the Services page or contact us to start a conversation.

Where can I buy the novellas?

All titles are available through the Shop on Gumroad. Sagas of the Ronin Monks is the best entry point for new readers.

Do I need to know the lore to enjoy the books?

No. Each novella works as a standalone entry point. The Sky Khan Doctrines page provides optional context for readers who want to go deeper.

Where is the music?

Stream on the music page via SoundCloud, or buy directly on Bandcamp. The Static Before Sound album pairs directly with the novellas.

How do I contact the studio?

Use the contact form. For service inquiries, describe which service you need and your project. We respond within 3 business days.
