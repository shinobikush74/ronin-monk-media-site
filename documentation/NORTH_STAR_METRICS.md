## PURPOSE

North Star Metrics answer one question each morning:

> **Is the Creative Order advancing?**

Not growing fast enough. Not hitting industry benchmarks. Not impressing anyone. Advancing. Progress against yesterday. These eight metrics are the only numbers that matter in Phase IV.

---

## THE EIGHT METRICS

### 1. Monthly Revenue
**Definition:** Total revenue received in the current calendar month  
**Target (Phase I):** $350/month  
**Tracked In:** Revenue Command, Revenue Center  
**Updated:** As transactions clear  
**Why It Matters:** The primary mission of Phase IV is revenue. If this number is not moving, something in the system is broken.

---

### 2. Qualified Leads
**Definition:** Prospective clients who have completed an initial Discovery conversation  
**Target (Phase I):** 3 active qualified leads at all times  
**Tracked In:** Client Command, CRM  
**Updated:** After each Discovery Call  
**Why It Matters:** Leads that haven't been qualified are hopes. Leads that have been qualified are opportunities. This number tells us how many real opportunities exist.

---

### 3. Proposal Conversion Rate
**Definition:** (Proposals Won ÷ Total Proposals Sent) × 100  
**Target (Phase I):** 40%+  
**Tracked In:** Proposal Command  
**Updated:** Monthly  
**Why It Matters:** Conversion rate reveals whether the proposals are landing. A low rate means price, positioning, or follow-up needs adjustment. A high rate with low volume means we need more leads.

---

### 4. Collector Growth
**Definition:** Net new collectors added this calendar month (first-time purchasers of any artifact)  
**Target (Phase I):** 5 new collectors/month  
**Tracked In:** Collector Command  
**Updated:** Weekly  
**Why It Matters:** Collectors are the foundation of the Patron, Member, and Builder pipeline. Every first sale begins a relationship. Collector growth is audience compound interest.

---

### 5. Publishing Streak
**Definition:** Consecutive calendar days with at least one piece of content published on any platform  
**Target (Phase I):** Unbroken streak  
**Tracked In:** Creator Command, Publishing Center  
**Updated:** Daily  
**Why It Matters:** The Publishing Streak enforces the Daily Builder Principle. Publishing creates visibility. Visibility creates opportunity. An unbroken streak means the system is functioning.

---

### 6. Creator Hours Saved
**Definition:** Hours per week the Creator does not spend on administrative, research, or organizational tasks due to agent delegation  
**Target (Phase I):** 3+ hours/week  
**Tracked In:** Creator Command  
**Updated:** Weekly estimate  
**Why It Matters:** Every hour saved by an agent is an hour the Creator can spend on Revenue, Creation, or Relationships. Agent ROI is measured in Creator hours recovered.

---

### 7. Repeat Clients
**Definition:** Total number of clients who have returned for a second or subsequent engagement  
**Target (Phase I):** 2 repeat clients within 90 days of first sale  
**Tracked In:** CRM, Client Command  
**Updated:** When engagement confirmed  
**Why It Matters:** Repeat clients are the most efficient path to revenue. No outreach required. Trust already established. A repeat client is proof the work delivers real value.

---

### 8. Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
**Definition:** Total confirmed revenue expected monthly from recurring sources (retainers, subscriptions, memberships)  
**Target (Phase I):** $100 MRR  
**Tracked In:** Revenue Command, Revenue Center  
**Updated:** When recurring agreements change  
**Why It Matters:** MRR is the most stable form of income. It compounds. One retainer client changes the financial baseline. MRR is how Ronin Monk Media becomes financially durable.

## DAILY REVIEW PROTOCOL

Each morning during the Daily Council Brief, review these five metrics (full 8-metric review is weekly):

| Morning Check | Full Weekly |
|---------------|-------------|
| Monthly Revenue | All 8 metrics |
| Publishing Streak | Conversion Rate trend |
| Qualified Leads count | MRR trend |
| Collector count change | Creator Hours Saved |
| One Opportunity Radar signal | Repeat Client progress |

---

## METRIC HEALTH INDICATORS

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **On Track** | Within 10% of target or better |
| **Watch** | 10–25% below target |
| **Action Required** | More than 25% below target or stagnant for 14+ days |
| **Critical** | Zero movement for 30+ days |
