## INTERNATIONAL CLIENT ACQUISITION APPROACH

### Step 1 — Find the Communities First

Before direct outreach to any international market, spend 2 weeks in the communities:
- Find Facebook groups and Instagram accounts for creative entrepreneurs in the target country
- Find English-language communities where the target market is active (many Nigerian, Ghanaian, and South African creative professionals use English as a primary professional language)
- Find Afrofuturism and worldbuilding communities — these are already international

### Step 2 — Lead with the Universe

The Sky Khan universe is the best introduction to international markets, especially on the African continent and in the diaspora. The Afrofuturist tradition resonates in Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, the UK, and France before a consulting pitch ever lands.

**International outreach sequence:**
1. Share lore / artifacts first (Entry Signal is free — no friction)
2. Engage in community discussions about Afrofuturism and worldbuilding
3. Introduce consulting work as an extension of the creative philosophy

### Step 3 — Adapt the Message, Not the Standard

The transformation philosophy stays constant across all markets:
- We sell transformation, not deliverables
- We do not compete on price
- We serve clients with real work and real community

What adapts:
- **Platform** (WhatsApp is primary in Nigeria and Ghana; adapt accordingly)
- **Examples** (use locally relevant business types in audits)
- **Timing** (UK clients are 5 hours ahead; Nigerian clients are 6 hours ahead)
- **Payment** (international transfers — Wise or Payoneer; state this clearly in proposals)

## INTERNATIONAL PAYMENT INFRASTRUCTURE

**Accepted international payment methods:**
- PayPal (available globally)
- Wise / TransferWise (preferred for Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, UK, EU)
- Payoneer (Africa-specific alternative)
- Stripe (if set up) — available in most Tier 1 markets

**Currency policy:**  
Quote in USD. Accept payment in USD. This protects against exchange rate variance and keeps the business simple.

**Exception:** UK clients may be quoted in GBP at the Creator's discretion. Convert at the time of proposal.

## AFROFUTURISM AS GLOBAL MARKET ENTRY

The Sky Khan universe is proof of concept for an international audience:

- The Afrofuturist tradition has active, growing communities in Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, the UK, France, and Brazil
- The music (Dee Lite, Frequency Wars) connects across the African diaspora
- The lore (Codex, Violet Oath) resonates with readers who are used to being excluded from the dominant fantasy genre

**Every international outreach opportunity should begin with the work, not the business card.**
