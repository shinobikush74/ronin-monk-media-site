## OUTREACH LAW

Personal beats broadcast. One genuine message to the right person outperforms a hundred mass posts. Quality of contact determines quality of client. Every outreach carries the weight of the brand.

## PROSPECT PRIORITIZATION

### Tier 1 — Warm Network (Highest Conversion)
Former clients, collaborators, mentors, or anyone who has already seen the quality of the work.

**Approach:** Direct. Personal. Reference shared history.  
**Expected response rate:** 30–50%

### Tier 2 — Creator Network (High Alignment)
Other creators — writers, musicians, visual artists, worldbuilders — who would benefit from consulting services or who could connect with the artifacts.

**Approach:** Lead with the work, not the pitch. Peer energy.  
**Expected response rate:** 15–25%

### Tier 3 — Business Network (High Revenue Potential)
Small business owners, startups, brand builders who need narrative identity work.

**Approach:** Lead with their problem. Signal Calibration and Brand Identity angles.  
**Expected response rate:** 10–20%

### Tier 4 — Cold Discovery (Volume Play)
Inbound from social content, community members, anyone discovering the brand cold.

**Approach:** Respond to every comment and DM within 24 hours. Don't pitch — connect.  
**Expected response rate:** 5–15%

## OUTREACH RULES

1. **Every message is personal.** No mass sends. No blast emails. No copy-paste without customization.
2. **Lead with their world, not yours.** Reference their work before mentioning yours.
3. **One ask per message.** Don't propose three things. Propose one next step.
4. **No follow-up wars.** Two follow-ups maximum before archiving. Dignity protects the brand.
5. **Every "no" is a gift.** A clear no frees time for a better prospect. Thank them.
6. **Creator sends everything.** Proposal Agent prepares — Creator reads and sends. Nothing goes out without Creator eyes.
