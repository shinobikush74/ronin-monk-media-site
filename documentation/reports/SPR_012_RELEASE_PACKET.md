> The system serves the Creator.
> The Creator does not serve the system.
>
> If any component of ROS requires more effort to maintain than it delivers in value,
> that component shall be simplified or removed.
>
> The measure of ROS is not its complexity.
> The measure of ROS is the Creative Order's ability to earn, create, serve, and remember.
