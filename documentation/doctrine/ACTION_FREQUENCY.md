## I. THE FIFTH FREQUENCY DOCTRINE

> The Headquarters no longer ends with consumption.
> It ends with participation.

The first four frequencies transmit:
- **WORD** transmits ideas
- **SOUND** transmits frequency
- **SYMBOL** transmits memory
- **STORY** transmits the universe

**ACTION** is what happens when the transmission lands.

ACTION is not a fifth medium. It is what the other four produce in the world — the change that happens in a person who has truly received the signal.

The Fifth Frequency is where:
- Doctrine becomes daily practice
- Story becomes personal mythology
- Community becomes Order
- Each one teaches one
- Each one reaches one

## VI. THE EACH ONE TEACH ONE / EACH ONE REACH ONE PROTOCOLS

These are the two core transmission mechanisms of the Fifth Frequency.

### Each One Teach One
Every person who completes a Ronin Monk Media service or workshop is asked one question:
> *Who in your life is where you were before this began?*

They are offered a tool to share what they received — not the deliverable, but the experience. A letter. A prompt. A recommendation with context. The mechanism is not automated. It is intentional.

### Each One Reach One
Every Order member receives, at a specific milestone (3 months in, or after a completed Creative Mission), a transmission designed to be forwarded. It is not a referral link. It is a letter — written by the Creator — addressed to "the person you know who is ready to receive this." The carrier decides whether to send it and to whom.

The frequency spreads because of the quality of what is transmitted, not because of a marketing system.

## VII. THE METRIC FOR THE FIFTH FREQUENCY

The Fifth Frequency is not measured by conversion rate, click-through rate, or engagement time.

It is measured by:
- **Creative Missions submitted** — how many people acted
- **Each One Teach One completions** — how many people shared
- **Each One Reach One transmissions** — how many people carried the signal to someone new
- **Workshop completions** — how many people finished the transformation they started
- **Volunteer Project hours** — how many hours went toward the world that should exist
