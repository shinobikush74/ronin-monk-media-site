## THE PRINCIPLE OF RESONANCE

Truth is recognized by its resonance,

not by its origin.

Wisdom may come from any worthy source.

The Ronin Monk studies broadly while remaining centered.

We inherit wisdom without inheriting its chains.

We honor traditions.

We preserve what serves humanity.

We release what no longer does.

We are students of many paths,

yet servants of none.

## THE REVENUE PRINCIPLE

A Ronin Monk builds for generations,

but earns in the present.

Every creation must strengthen the future while sustaining the work today.

Every sprint must accomplish at least one of the following:

- Increase revenue.
- Increase audience.
- Strengthen the Sky Khan intellectual property.
- Save the Creator meaningful time.

If it accomplishes none of these, it belongs in the backlog.
