## MISSION

Build a permanent audience of collectors who believe in the Ronin Monk Media universe — the stories, the music, the lore, and the world of Sky Khan.

Collectors are not customers. Customers complete transactions. Collectors join a creative order. The goal is not to sell products. The goal is to find the people whose lives are enriched by what this brand creates — and give them a way to show it.

---

## THE COLLECTOR LADDER

Every person who encounters the brand is on the Collector Ladder. The ladder has six tiers. Movement is always upward.

```
Visitors → Readers → Collectors → Patrons → Members → Builders
```

| Tier | Definition | Conversion Rate | Primary Action |
|------|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Visitors** | Arrived at Headquarters | 3–5% become Readers | Create compelling entry points |
| **Readers** | Engaged with content (lore, archive, music) | 10–15% become Collectors | Deliver undeniable value |
| **Collectors** | Purchased at least one artifact | 20% become Patrons | Follow up, deliver more |
| **Patrons** | Repeat buyers, 2+ artifacts owned | 30% become Members | Invite into deeper relationship |
| **Members** | The Order — recurring supporters | 15% become Builders | Serve them like family |
| **Builders** | Active advocates and collaborators | Each one reach one | Give them something worth sharing |

## ENTRY POINT STRATEGY

The first artifact a collector receives must be worth more than they paid for it. The goal is not to extract value. The goal is to create an evangelist.

### Entry Signal (Free)
The free product is the most important product. It introduces the universe, demonstrates quality, and gives the visitor a reason to return.

**Rules for the Entry Signal:**
- Must stand alone as a complete piece of creative work
- Must reference the larger universe (tease the Codex, the Frequency Wars, Sky Khan)
- Must include a clear next step (link to Codex, email list, or social)

### First Paid Artifact ($5 — Codex of the Waste-Born)
The $5 price removes friction and establishes the buyer as a collector. The experience of receiving and consuming the Codex determines whether they return.

**Rules for first paid artifacts:**
- Delivery must be immediate and frictionless
- Quality must exceed what the price suggests
- A follow-up communication should go out within 7 days

## PROMOTION PRINCIPLES

### The Scarcity Principle
Limited edition artifacts drive urgency. "20 copies remaining" is more compelling than "available now." Where genuine scarcity exists, communicate it clearly and honestly.

### The Story First Principle
No collector buys a product description. They buy the world it comes from. Every promotion leads with story: a line from the Codex, a scene from the Frequency Wars, an image from the Sky Khan universe. The product is proof that the world is real.

### The Platform Principle
Where promotion happens determines who sees it. In Phase IV, the primary platforms are:

| Platform | Content Type | Audience |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Primary social (Creator's choice) | Story excerpts, lore drops, character art | New visitors |
| Gumroad | Product pages, email list | Readers and buyers |
| Bandcamp | Music, audio | Music-first audience |
| Headquarters (website) | Full lore archive, blog | Loyal readers |

## COLLECTOR COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

Every collector deserves to feel like they are part of something real.

### After First Purchase
- Confirm delivery (automated via platform)
- Optional: personal message from the Creator within 72 hours
- Introduce next artifact in the sequence

### After Second Purchase (Patron threshold)
- Acknowledge the repeat commitment
- Give access to something not publicly available (a draft chapter, an early look)
- Begin treating them as Members in waiting

### For Members
- They receive updates before the public
- Their feedback is solicited on new work
- They are acknowledged when they advocate for the brand

## THE EMAIL LIST IMPERATIVE

Path 4 to revenue (Passive Income) requires an email list. All other paths work without it. But the email list is the only asset Ronin Monk Media owns outright — no algorithm controls it.

Every piece of content, every product page, and every social profile should have one clear call to action:

> **Join the Order. Get the Entry Signal free.**

Phase I goal: **10 email subscribers**  
Phase II goal: **100 subscribers**  
Path 4 becomes viable at approximately 500 engaged subscribers.
