## THE FOUR SPRINT QUESTIONS

Every sprint proposal must answer all four before implementation begins:

1. **30/60/90 days:** How will this increase revenue within the next 30, 60, or 90 days?
2. **Audience:** How will this increase audience growth?
3. **IP:** How will this strengthen the value of the Sky Khan intellectual property?
4. **Automation:** How will this reduce the Creator's workload through automation or systems?

Every sprint must produce at least one measurable business outcome.
