# EXECUTIVE COUNCIL
## Ronin Operating System v1.0 — Permanent Council Structure
**Established:** 2026-06-26
**Status:** Active — governs organizational thinking and recommendation

---

## THE COUNCIL PRINCIPLE

> The Councils think.
> The Agents execute.
> The Creator decides.

The Executive Council is not a committee. It does not have meetings. It does not vote. Each Council is a structured domain of organizational thinking — a lens through which one dimension of the Creative Order is continuously monitored, analyzed, and reported.

Every Council:
- Has a defined domain of responsibility
- Has a specific agent (or agents) that serve it
- Produces regular outputs for the Daily Council Brief
- Escalates decisions to the Creator — never acts unilaterally
- Measures its own health by the ROS Objectives

---

## THE SIX COUNCILS

---

### 1. REVENUE COUNCIL

**Domain:** All income generation, pipeline management, and financial health of the Creative Order.

**Mission:** Ensure that every day moves the Creative Order measurably toward financial sustainability and long-term growth.

**Governing document:** `REVENUE_CONSTITUTION.md`

**Primary agent:** Chief Revenue Officer (CRO Agent)

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor all three pipelines (Sales, Collector, Client) daily
- Identify the Next $100 Mission each morning
- Track progress toward Phase I ($100K) / Phase II ($350K) / Phase III ($1M+)
- Flag stalled prospects and recommend re-engagement actions
- Track product performance and surface highest-converting artifacts
- Recommend pricing, positioning, and timing for new products and services
- Monitor revenue by stream and flag underperforming streams
- Brief the Creator on the highest-probability revenue action of the day

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- Revenue yesterday / month / year
- Pipeline value
- Consultations pending
- Next $100 Mission
- Top recommendation

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A proposal is ready for review
- A prospect has gone cold after 7+ days
- A revenue stream drops or surges unexpectedly
- A new revenue opportunity is identified

---

### 2. PUBLISHING COUNCIL

**Domain:** All transmissions — content creation, scheduling, platform adaptation, and publication across all channels.

**Mission:** One creation. Infinite reach. Every approved transmission reaches every relevant platform in the right format for that platform's audience.

**Primary agent:** Publishing Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain publishing queue (drafts awaiting Creator approval)
- Transform approved content into platform-specific formats
- Track publication schedule and cadence per platform
- Monitor engagement signals per platform and surface to Creator
- Ensure all published content aligns with the Doctrine of Remembrance
- Never publish without explicit Creator approval
- Adapt language and format for international markets where directed

**Platform coverage:**
Website (Archive) · YouTube · Instagram · Facebook · LinkedIn · X · Bandcamp · Wavlake · Fountain · Primal · Newsletter (The Order)

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- Content in queue (awaiting Creator approval)
- Published today / this week
- Platform engagement summary
- Recommended next publication

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A draft is ready for review
- An engagement signal is unusually high or low
- A platform policy changes that affects publishing
- A collaboration or cross-promotion opportunity is identified

---

### 3. CREATIVE COUNCIL

**Domain:** The Sky Khan intellectual property, lore development, worldbuilding, and the creative direction of all Ronin Monk Media output.

**Mission:** Deepen the mythology. Protect the canon. Ensure every creation strengthens the world.

**Primary agent:** None (Creator-led; Archive Agent supports)

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain the integrity of Sky Khan canon
- Surface lore development opportunities (new factions, characters, locations, artifacts)
- Review all publicly-facing creative content for canon consistency
- Identify IP licensing and expansion opportunities
- Brief the Creator on creative direction choices that require a decision
- Maintain the Creative Order's artistic standards per the Worldbuilding Style Guide

**Operating principle:** The Creative Council is the most Creator-dependent of all councils. No canon decisions are made without the Creator. The council's role is to surface options, protect existing canon, and flag conflicts — never to create or modify lore autonomously.

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- IP status summary
- Pending creative decisions
- Lore development opportunities
- Canon integrity alerts

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A new creative product is proposed
- A canon conflict is detected
- An IP licensing opportunity emerges
- A collaboration would affect canon

---

### 4. OPERATIONS COUNCIL

**Domain:** The health and continuous improvement of all operational systems — the Headquarters, RMHQ, ROS components, and the technical foundation.

**Mission:** The systems work. The Creator's time is protected. Everything runs.

**Primary agent:** None (Claude as Editor serves this function)

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor the health of the Headquarters (broken links, missing assets, performance)
- Maintain the RMHQ admin system
- Track and prioritize technical debt
- Evaluate new sprint proposals against the Revenue Constitution sprint test
- Maintain the sprint log and execution record
- Ensure all operations align with the Constitution and Revenue Constitution
- Monitor security (CSP, admin protection, protected IP)

**Operating principle:** The Operations Council does not initiate projects. It evaluates proposals and maintains what exists. New sprints are proposed by other councils and approved by the Creator after passing the Revenue Constitution test.

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- System health status
- Open sprint proposals
- Technical debt items flagged
- Security status

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A system failure affects the public site or admin
- A security issue is detected
- A sprint proposal passes the Revenue Constitution test and is ready for Creator decision
- A dependency or infrastructure change is required

---

### 5. INTELLIGENCE COUNCIL

**Domain:** Market intelligence, audience intelligence, and competitive landscape. The eyes and ears of the Creative Order in the wider world.

**Mission:** The Creative Order always knows where opportunity is emerging before the competition.

**Primary agent:** Market Intelligence Agent

**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor the Afrofuturism creative space daily
- Monitor underground Hip Hop and beat culture
- Monitor speculative fiction, fantasy, and anime communities
- Monitor worldbuilding and tabletop RPG communities
- Monitor collector markets (limited editions, creator-economy products)
- Monitor key international markets (US / UK / Nigeria / Ghana / South Africa / Japan / South Korea)
- Monitor emerging platforms and distribution channels
- Produce Daily Intelligence Brief
- Flag opportunities, threats, and partnerships to the Creator

**The Intelligence Standard:**
Intelligence is only valuable if it is specific, timely, and actionable. The Market Intelligence Agent does not produce general summaries. It identifies specific opportunities — a community trend, a collaboration target, a platform shift — with a clear recommended action.

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- Top market signal (one finding, one action)
- Platform shifts or policy changes
- Emerging opportunity (if any)
- Threat or risk (if any)

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A major platform shift affects the Creative Order's publishing strategy
- A collaboration or partnership opportunity is identified
- A competitor or influencer in the space makes a move that warrants a response
- An international market shows unusual activity

---

### 6. ARCHIVE COUNCIL

**Domain:** All knowledge preservation, document governance, and the long-term institutional memory of the Creative Order.

**Mission:** The Order never forgets.

**Primary agent:** None (managed through documentation system and Claude as Editor)

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain all documentation per the established file structure
- Ensure all doctrine documents are current and accurate
- Maintain REPORT_INDEX.md and PROJECT_EXECUTION_LOG.md
- Archive all sprint decisions and rationale
- Surface relevant historical decisions when they bear on current choices
- Maintain the Sky Khan lore archive (Lore Engine)
- Ensure knowledge is accessible for future creators who inherit the Order

**Operating principle:** The Archive Council is stewardship, not creation. It preserves and organizes what the Creator creates. The constitution can be amended but the log is append-only. The past is honored by recording it accurately.

**Daily output for Council Brief:**
- Documents requiring update
- Archive items flagged for Creator attention
- Knowledge gaps identified

**Escalates to Creator when:**
- A document conflict is detected (two documents contradict each other)
- A decision log entry requires Creator review
- A lore canon item requires archiving

## THE DAILY COUNCIL BRIEF ROLE

Each Council contributes to the Daily Council Brief:
- Revenue Council → revenue status + Next $100 Mission
- Publishing Council → queue status + top recommendation
- Creative Council → pending creative decisions
- Operations Council → system health + sprint status
- Intelligence Council → top market signal
- Archive Council → documents requiring action

The Creator reviews the consolidated brief each morning. This replaces 5–7 separate reviews with one integrated decision surface.
