## THE FOUR PATHS TO THE NEXT $100

Every iteration of the Next $100 Mission uses one or more of these four paths:

### Path 1 — Consulting (Highest ROI per hour)
One World Awakening session ($125) closes the gap immediately.

**Required actions:**
1. Identify 5 warm contacts (people who know you, respect your thinking, have a creative project)
2. Write one personalized message per contact — not a broadcast, not a pitch
3. Message centers on their situation, not the service
4. Offer one specific session with one specific outcome
5. Follow up in 48–72 hours if no response

**Close rate target:** 1 in 5 warm contacts says yes
**Time to $100:** 1–7 days
**Creator time required:** 2–3 hours outreach + 1 hour session

---

### Path 2 — Digital Artifacts (Volume play)
20 Codex of the Waste-Born sales at $5 = $100. 10 Initiation Protocol sales at $10 = $100. 7 Frequency Wars sales at $15 = $105.

**Required actions:**
1. Post the artifact with scarcity language (Codex: "20 copies remaining")
2. Frame as artifact — not "buy my ebook"
3. Post on 2–3 platforms with different audiences
4. Include the doctrine context — why this exists, what it carries

**Close rate target:** 3–5% of reach converts
**Time to $100:** 1–14 days depending on reach
**Creator time required:** 1 hour per promotion post

## THE PIPELINE THAT FEEDS THE PROTOCOL

The Next $100 Protocol draws its data from the three operational pipelines:

**Sales Pipeline** (Awareness → Advocate):
- CRO Agent scans for prospects in Conversation and Proposal stages
- These are the most likely to convert today
- Protocol prioritizes moving these forward over finding new leads

**Collector Pipeline** (Visitors → Builders):
- Promotion actions (Path 2) move Visitors to Collectors
- Existing Readers are targeted before cold audiences
- Protocol identifies which tier has the most people closest to the next tier

**Client Pipeline** (Inquiry → Referral):
- Stalled clients in Proposal or Booked stages represent recovered revenue
- Protocol flags these for follow-up before new outreach begins
- A recovered stalled proposal is faster than a new lead

## THE PROTOCOL RULE

> Before reaching for new leads, close what is already open.

The pipeline always has a highest-probability close. The protocol identifies it. The Creator acts on it.

New outreach only begins when:
1. No prospects are in Conversation or Proposal stages
2. No proposals are outstanding for more than 14 days without response
3. No clients are in Project Active stage awaiting Creator input
