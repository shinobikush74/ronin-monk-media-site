## THE PRINCIPLE

The market discovers expertise before it sees pricing.

A prospect who has already read your breakdown of a church website, watched your brand audit video, or learned something useful from your educational post does not need to be sold. They need to be invited.

**Publish 1 authority piece per day. Every piece answers a question the ideal client is already asking.**

---

## CONTENT TYPES

### 1. Website Audits (Public)
Break down a real website publicly — use a business type from the target list, with no client names. Show the gap between what they have and what's possible.

**Format:** LinkedIn post or short article (400–600 words)  
**Frequency:** 2x per week  
**Template:**
> I spent 5 minutes on [type of business]'s website. Here's what I found — and what one change would do for their business.
> [3 observations, 1 highest-impact recommendation]
> This is the kind of work I do for clients. If your [website / brand / platform] deserves a look, DM me.

---

### 2. Brand Breakdowns
Analyze a brand's visual identity, voice, or positioning. Not to criticize — to teach. Show the principles at work.

**Format:** Instagram carousel (5–7 slides) or LinkedIn article  
**Frequency:** 1x per week  
**Angle options:**
- "Why [Type of Business] needs a brand story, not just a logo"
- "Three signs your brand is underselling your business"
- "What the best independent musicians do with their brand identity"

---

### 3. Before / After Examples
With client permission, document the transformation. Before the project, after the project, the business impact.

**Format:** Instagram/LinkedIn post with images  
**Frequency:** As clients are delivered  
**Template:**
> Before: [2 sentences on the starting state]  
> After: [2 sentences on the delivered state]  
> What changed: [1 business outcome]

---

### 4. Educational Articles
Short, specific, opinionated pieces on topics the ideal client is thinking about.

**Format:** LinkedIn article or Substack post (500–1,000 words)  
**Frequency:** 1x per week  
**Topic bank:**
- "Why your church's website is costing you new members"
- "The three things every independent author's platform needs"
- "What a barbershop's brand should say before the first haircut"
- "Why 'affordable' is the most expensive word in your business"
- "The difference between a website and a digital presence"
- "How to build a collector ecosystem for your creative work"
- "Why your Instagram following isn't your business"

---

### 5. Short Videos / Screen Recordings
Record your screen while walking through a website or brand audit. No editing required. Just commentary.

**Format:** YouTube, Instagram Reels, Facebook  
**Frequency:** 1x per week  
**Format:**
- Under 3 minutes
- "I found this [type of business]'s website and I have to show you what I see"
- Specific, kind, actionable

---

### 6. Lore / Sky Khan Universe Content
Content that demonstrates the depth and intentionality of the work — the Sky Khan universe is proof of concept for what Ronin Monk Media builds for clients.

**Format:** Instagram, X, Facebook  
**Frequency:** 2–3x per week  
**Types:**
- Lore fragments and world excerpts
- "How I built the Sky Khan universe" process posts
- Connections between the lore and the consulting work
- Artifact promotions (Codex, Violet Oath, Frequency Wars)

---

### 7. Client Success Stories
After delivery and with permission: tell the story of the transformation.

**Format:** LinkedIn, website testimonials page  
**Frequency:** As earned  
**Structure:**
- Who the client was (without identifying details if they prefer)
- What they started with
- What was built
- What changed for their business

## CONTENT RULES

1. **Every piece answers a question the ideal client is already asking.** Not what you want to say — what they need to hear.
2. **Specific beats general.** "Three things every barbershop's website needs" outperforms "The importance of good branding."
3. **No humble-bragging.** Demonstrate expertise through substance, not self-promotion.
4. **One CTA per piece, maximum.** Pick one: DM me, link in bio, reply to this post. Not all three.
5. **Consistency outperforms virality.** Show up every day. One piece a day for 90 days changes perception.
6. **The lore is proof.** Sky Khan is the world's best portfolio piece for the worldbuilding and brand architecture services. Use it.
