## COMPLETE EXAMPLE PROPOSAL — Church Digital Transformation

---

**TRANSFORMATION PROPOSAL**  
New Covenant Community Church  
Prepared by Ronin Monk Media LLC  
Date: 2026-06-26  
Proposal ID: PRO-2026-001

---

**CURRENT STATE**

New Covenant Community Church has served the Atlanta community for 23 years. The congregation is active, the mission is clear, and the Facebook Page reflects a community that genuinely cares for each other. By every in-person measure, this is a healthy and growing church.

The digital presence, however, does not yet reflect the church's strength. The current website was last updated in 2023 and leads with an archived sermon rather than a welcome to first-time visitors. There is no online giving option — a gap that affects both existing members who prefer digital and potential new members who look for digital giving as a signal of organizational modernity. The site is not optimized for mobile, and more than 70% of church website visits happen on phones.

This is not a criticism of the church's commitment. It is an observation that the digital front door does not yet match the warmth of the physical one.

---

**FUTURE STATE**

New Covenant's website becomes the first point of contact for anyone in the Atlanta area who is searching for a church. It welcomes them with the pastor's voice, with service times that are impossible to miss, with stories of what the community has built together.

Members who want to give can do so in 30 seconds from their phone. Visitors who discover the church at 11pm on a Tuesday can find exactly what they need without making a phone call. Young adults searching for a church that feels alive can see, in the first 10 seconds, that New Covenant is exactly that.

The digital presence becomes a mission tool — extending the church's reach into the community beyond Sunday morning.

---

**THE TRANSFORMATION**

**Phase 1 — Foundation**
- Brand audit: review all existing digital materials and document the current state
- Mission and vision alignment session (30 minutes with the pastor)
- Digital giving integration: Tithe.ly setup, QR code generation, placement on all pages
- Google Business optimization: current photos, service times, mission statement

**Phase 2 — Build**
- New website: 5 pages (Home, I'm New Here, Our Story, Events, Give)
- Mobile-first design throughout
- Sermon archive integration (existing sermons organized and accessible)
- Event calendar with Google Calendar sync
- Contact form with auto-response

**Phase 3 — Launch**
- Staff training (1 hour): how to update the site, post events, access giving dashboard
- Launch announcement assets for Facebook and email
- 30-day post-launch check-in

---

**TIMELINE**

Phase 1: Foundation — 1 week  
Phase 2: Build — 3 weeks  
Phase 3: Launch — 1 week  
**Total: 5 weeks from signed agreement to launch**

---

**INVESTMENT**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Phase 1: Foundation & Digital Giving Setup | $400 |
| Phase 2: 5-Page Website Design & Build | $900 |
| Phase 3: Launch & Training | $200 |
| **Total** | **$1,500** |

**Payment Structure:**
- $600 at signing (40%)
- $450 at Phase 2 delivery (30%)
- $450 at launch (30%)

---

**EXPECTED OUTCOMES**

1. Digital giving option live within 2 weeks of signing — potential immediate impact on weekly offering
2. New visitor conversion: a clear "I'm New Here" path converts online curiosity into first-time attendance
3. Mobile experience fixed — 70%+ of website visits now reach a working, welcoming page
4. Google search visibility improved — church appears for "[denomination] church [city]" searches
5. Staff empowered to update the site without technical help
