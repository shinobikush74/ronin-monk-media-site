## PUBLISHING LAW

One creation. Three platforms minimum. Every post has a purpose beyond the post — it builds the audience that buys the next thing. Publishing Agent adapts content — Creator approves before posting.

---

## THE ONE-TO-MANY RULE

Every piece of content created must produce:
- **1 original piece** (the source)
- **3 platform-adapted posts minimum**
- **1 link back** to Gumroad or Headquarters in every post

## CONTENT TYPES AND PLATFORM FIT

### Lore Excerpts
- **Best platforms:** Instagram (graphic), X (thread), Facebook (post), YouTube (reading)
- **CTA:** "Read the full text at [Gumroad]"
- **Revenue connection:** Direct link to Codex, Violet Oath, or Frequency Wars

### Music
- **Best platforms:** Bandcamp (full release), YouTube (visual), Instagram (Reel clip), X (clip + quote)
- **CTA:** "The world this music comes from — [artifact link]"
- **Revenue connection:** Music → lore → artifact purchase

### Worldbuilding Insights
- **Best platforms:** LinkedIn (professional framing), X (thread), YouTube (explainer)
- **CTA:** "I help brands build worlds like this — World Awakening consultation"
- **Revenue connection:** Direct consulting inquiry

### Service Stories / Case Studies
- **Best platforms:** LinkedIn (professional), Facebook (community), Instagram (carousel)
- **CTA:** Service name and price + "DM to start"
- **Revenue connection:** Direct consulting

### Creator Journey Updates
- **Best platforms:** X, Instagram Stories, Facebook
- **CTA:** "Follow the build"
- **Revenue connection:** Builds trust → all revenue paths

## CONTENT RULES

1. **Every post has a purpose.** Visibility, trust, or revenue — ideally all three.
2. **Story before sale.** Lead with the world, the insight, the moment. Link comes at the end.
3. **No platform gets the same post.** Adapt tone and format for each audience.
4. **Quality beats frequency.** A weak post that goes out is worse than a strong post that is one day late.
5. **Publishing Agent adapts — Creator approves.** No post goes live without Creator's eyes on it.

---

## PUBLISHING STREAK TRACKING

The Publishing Streak counts every calendar day with at least one post on any platform.

**Minimum to count:** One post, on one platform, with a link back to a Headquarters page or Gumroad.

**Streak resets if:** A full calendar day passes with zero posts on any tracked platform.
