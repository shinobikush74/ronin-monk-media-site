## III. THE THREE DOCTRINES

### The Doctrine of Remembrance
> Ronin Monk Media is not a creative studio. It is a place of remembrance.
> Every story is a weapon against hopelessness.
> Every symbol is a reminder of forgotten greatness.
> Every song is a transmission.
> Enter not to consume, but to remember.

### The Second Doctrine
> Remember who you were.
> Imagine who you could become.
> Build what the world has forgotten.

### The Doctrine of Resonance
> A Ronin Monk does not seek one truth.
> A Ronin Monk seeks the harmony between truths.
> Every worthy tradition offers a fragment.
> Creation is the art of bringing those fragments into resonance.

---

## IV. THE RONIN METHOD

All creative work at Ronin Monk Media follows this method:

1. **Observe deeply** — understand the source material, the person, the world
2. **Study widely** — draw from many traditions and disciplines
3. **Discern honestly** — separate what serves from what does not
4. **Integrate wisely** — bring the fragments into resonance
5. **Create courageously** — make the thing that must be made
6. **Serve humbly** — the creation is not for the creator
7. **Remember always** — every act of creation is an act of remembrance

---

## V. THE PURPOSE TEST

Before any creation, ask: does this help someone—
1. Remember who they are?
2. Imagine who they could become?
3. Build what the world has forgotten?

If none of these are true, the creation is out of alignment.

---

## VI. THE SPRINT TEST (REVENUE PRINCIPLE)

Before any sprint, ask: does this accomplish at least one of—
1. Increase revenue?
2. Increase audience?
3. Strengthen the Sky Khan intellectual property?
4. Save the Creator meaningful time?

If none of these are true, it belongs in the backlog.

---

## VII. THE ETHIC

> Leave every person, every place, every story, and every world
> better than you found it.

Applied:
- Every client engagement ends with the client more capable, not more dependent
- Every artifact carries something the recipient can use without Ronin Monk Media present
- Every collaboration leaves the collaborator with more than they brought
- Every digital location in the Headquarters is a place a visitor is glad they entered

---

## VIII. THE LENS

Afrofuturism is not a style. It is a method of seeing.

The ancestral and the futuristic are the same frequency at different distances.

Inner greatness is not earned. It is recovered.

**Applied:**
- The Afrofuturist lens comes before the medium
- Music, art, stories, cards, websites — these are expressions of one worldview
- Never separate the medium from the philosophy
- The lens is always visible in the work, but never announced

---

## IX. THE PRINCIPLE OF THE SAMPLE

Just as a Hip Hop producer transforms many recordings into one new composition,
the Ronin Monk transforms many streams of wisdom into one coherent expression.

**Applied:**
- Ronin Monk Media draws from African diasporic traditions, speculative fiction, martial philosophy, spiritual practice, creative entrepreneurship, and technology
- The source material is always honored — never appropriated without acknowledgment
- The final creation is always new — never derivative
- Wisdom from any worthy source is welcome. No tradition has a monopoly.

## X. THE CURRENT PHASE DIRECTIVE

*(From Constitution Version 1.0 — The Next Phase)*
